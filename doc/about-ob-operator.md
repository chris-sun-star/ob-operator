# About ob-operator

## What is ob-operator
Ob-operator is a powerful Kubernetes operator designed to automate and simplify the maintenance of OceanBase in your Kubernetes cluster.
OB-Operator leverages the capabilities of Kubernetes to streamline the management of OceanBase. By automating tasks such as deployment, scaling, backup and recovery, and rolling upgrades, OB-Operator eliminates the need for manual intervention, saving your valuable time and effort.

## Key Features of ob-operator
1. OceanBase cluster management, take cares of the whole lifecycle of OceanBase cluster, from deploy, scale, rolling upgrade, to destroy.
2. OceanBase tenant management, provides rich features to meet all the needs to operate a tenant in an OceanBase cluster.
3. Disaster recovery, always make the best effort to ensure your OceanBase cluster is healthy as desired.
4. Data backup and restore, act as the final guard of you data.
